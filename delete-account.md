# Delete Your Laktic Account

_Last updated: 2026-05-07_

This page describes how to request deletion of your Laktic Running account and associated data.

## How to request account deletion

To delete your Laktic Running account and all associated data, email us:

- **Email:** travis.william.mahoney@gmail.com
- **Subject:** Delete my Laktic account
- **Include in the email body:**
  - The email address you used to sign up for Laktic
  - A brief confirmation that you want your account permanently deleted

We will process your deletion request and confirm by email within **7 business days**.

## What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is **permanently removed within 30 days**, with backup copies purged within 90 days:

- Your account (email, name, password)
- Your athlete profile (PRs, mileage, race calendar, training preferences)
- Your training plans
- Your coaching conversation history
- Your usage records (training plan generation count, coach message count)

## What is retained

The following data is retained for legal and compliance reasons:

- **Subscription and billing records** — retained for up to 7 years to comply with tax and consumer protection laws. These records do not include your training data.
- **Aggregate, anonymized analytics** — we may retain anonymized usage statistics that cannot be used to identify you (e.g., "X% of users completed onboarding").

## Questions

For any questions about account deletion or data retention, contact:
- **Email:** travis.william.mahoney@gmail.com
- **Subject:** Privacy Request — Laktic
